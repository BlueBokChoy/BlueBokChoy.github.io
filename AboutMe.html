About Me

Summary

Hello! My name is Roy and this is my portfolio. Below here, you can learn about me and the type of person I am, but here's a quick summary of it all.

I'm a person who likes to help others where I can. I'd also like to continue helping others into the future as my dream career is teaching. My bread and butter is my jokes (sometimes hit or miss..), they help me integrate and get to know my peers. I like to socialise where I can so jokes really help break the ice. I'm an avid fan of music too, and I play the trumpet myself. The music I like best is Jpop and swing (weird combination but trust me, it's good).
